Reap n' Sow

Game Modes:
- Easy Start: Stable environment, reap 3 plants = Victory.
- Drought Challenge: Includes drought events, reap 5 plants.
- Survival Challenge: Multiple events, reap 5 plants.

Controls:
- Movement: w=up, s=down, a=left, d=right
- Sow: "sow up/down/left/right"
- Reap: "reap up/down/left/right"
- Next Turn: 'n'
- Undo: 'undo'
- Redo: 'redo'
- Debug: 'debug'
- Save: 'save'
- Load: 'load'
- Quit: 'q' (no effect in browser)
